Colored Perspective

Much color.

Information!

Progress through an infinite terrain collecting coins as you go that you can spend on upgrades. The cameras perspective changes constantly forcing you to guesstimate your timings and jumps adding an extra level of challenge.

Controls:

  • Swipe up to jump, tap to change color.
  • Tilt to move left or right, or hold down on either side of the screen to move that direction.(Configurable in options)
