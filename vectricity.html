Vectrcity is a game about the triangles and the cubicles, however there are others too. Use your choice of weapon against them along with whatever else you may happen to get.

Some basic info:

  • Left Mouse: Shoot
  • Q: Use Weapon Power
  • E: Buy Menu
  • Space: Sprint
  • Z: Place Tower
  • X: Place Freeze
  • C: Place Firetrap
  • V: Use Nuke
  • Esc: Pause/Exit Menu
