The basic controls:

  • Left Mouse: Shoot
  • Q: Use Weapon Power
  • E: Buy Menu
  • Space: Sprint
  • Z: Place Tower
  • X: Place Freeze
  • C: Place Firetrap
  • V: Use Time Bomb
  • Esc: Pause/Exit Menu
Play Now!

Powerup Upgrade Information:

  • Regeneration: +5 regeneration per second
  • Max Health: +25 max health
  • Upgrade Tower:+15 second duration
  • Upgrade Firewall: +15 second duration
  • Upgrade Freeze: +15 second duration
  • Upgrade Time Bomb: +.5 Sec slowdown

Mobile Controls:

  • Left side of screen: move
  • Right side of screen: rotate
  • double tap: place items
  • 3 finger tap: pause/upgrade menu
